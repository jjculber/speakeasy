Speakeasy Voice Chat

Welcome to Speakeasy Voice Chat.

Speakeasy is a Voice Messaging app designed for teams. Keep in touch with your friends, classmates, and coworkers. Enjoy the convienience of listening to messages when you want, while being able to express yourself vocally.
